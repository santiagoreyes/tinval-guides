# User Guide - English

## Getting Started

Welcome to the TIN Validation System.

### First Steps
1. Login with your credentials
2. Navigate to the dashboard
3. Select your validation method

## Single TIN Validation

**Purpose:** Validate individual taxpayer IDs.

### How to use:
1. Click "Single Validation"
2. Enter TIN: `XX-XXXXXX-X`
3. Click "Validate"

### Notes:
- Real-time validation
- Immediate results
- Max 1 TIN at a time
